Implementation Management

In the busy client environment where mission support and productivity are more critical than ever, employees who are qualified to oversee technology implementation efforts often lack the time or resources to helm such initiatives internally. An experienced third party is the best way to guarantee that such needs are satisfied. That is where Elucid Solutions steps into the picture.

PMBok Standards and PMP Competencies

For smaller agencies in particular, selecting COTS products is the best course of action when seeking to alleviate pain points and push job functions forward. Less costly and in need of little to no modification, a ready-made system can be tailored to an agency’s needs. To carry out these tasks effectively, Elucid draws on a blend of the PMBOK standards and PMP competencies, as these are proven, widely practiced methods for developing existing technologies in the workplace. The PMBOK, which is shaped by volunteer panels and the public, is structured around five process groups: initiating, planning, executing, monitoring and controlling, and closing. A compliment to that foundation, the PMP credential aims to ensure that project managers are equipped with the skills necessary to effectively administer endeavors regardless of agency or sector.
