Introduction

Elucid, as a leading IT consulting small business, was recently awarded the GSA 8(a) STARS II government-wide acquisition contract (GWAC). Our contract covers all four of the Functional Areas carved out in Constellation I, including:

  • CIFA1 - Custom Computer Programming Services,
  • CIFA2 - Computer Systems Design Services,
  • CIFA3 - Computer Facilities Management Services, and
  • CIFA4 - Other Computer Related Services.
